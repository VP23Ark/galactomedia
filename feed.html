What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to launch and grow your business

Advertising

We have people and techniques that specify the behaviour of the people using the platform to show them relevant and useful advertisements to help convert the newly found reach into newly found sales. Yeah, you read that right !!

Branding

We intend to use the Business Owner's personal vision for the product/service for the optimum branding / rebranding of the product to make it more relevant to the people viewing it. We offer the Business to be fully in control of the brand image or get to choose from the options specifically catering to the business and the customer.

Marketing

Businesses need marketing to get new sets of customers. We at GalactoMedia have a dedicated team with experience and expertise in SEO and SSM to help your brand to be seen by the masses on their phones. We know you know and we do it better than most people.

Influencers

We help influencers manage, grow and get more business opportunities by increasing their reach and connecting to their audiences better. We have management for the influencers dedicated to helping them shoot for the moon or at least land among the stars. And did we mention INCREASED REACH & BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES?

Events

We can help boost up your campaign by organizing very professional yet very flamboyant events like product launches, business person congregations, meet ups etc. Connect with us to have the time of your life while getting the business of your life.

Collaborations

We love the community. At Galacto Media we help brands collaborate with brands and influencers collaborate with influencers to join hands and help increase the reach while building long lasting relationships with users of their nichés respectively.
